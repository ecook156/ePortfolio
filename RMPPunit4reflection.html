Unit 4

End of Unit 4 Reflection

Although the module had previously covered ethics, this unit went more into detail on privacy and the legal aspects of data protection and how this can impact research being carried out. I found using a case study for seminar preparation helpful to practically apply the knowledge learnt through this unit to reflect on the data privacy impacts that could have an effect on the research carried out for the capstone project and ensuring confidentiality and consent throughout (Sim & Waterfield, 2019). Having a practical example to apply the theoretical knowledge helped with my understanding of the topic and will ensure I can apply the same thinking for any research I conduct that required contacting individuals.

Throughout this unit, I finalised my ideas for the literature review and did some research into the publicly available quantitative data that will be used as evidence in the discussion. I completed the literature review questions as an outline for submission to receive formative feedback.
