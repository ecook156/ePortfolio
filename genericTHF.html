The Human Factor

Learning Outcomes

1. Review the concepts and principles of human behaviour within the context of cyber security.

2. Develop an in-depth understanding of the elements of risk in a socio-technical system.

3. Critically examine effective strategies to promote and manage the human factor in cyber security.

4. Critically evaluate the social and ethical considerations in developing and applying usable security.
