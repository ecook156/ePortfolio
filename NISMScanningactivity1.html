Scanning Activity 1

Task

For this acitivty we were required to perform some basic scans on our assigned website using standard tools such as Traceroute to find out:

How many hops from your machine to your assigned website?

Which step causes the biggest delay in the route? What is the average duration of that delay?

What are the main nameservers for the website?

Who is the registered contact?

What is the MX record for the website?

Where is the website hosted?

Approach

For this exercise we decided as a group to each have a go at the scanning acitivy and then discuss and agree the results in our following meeting
