Group 2 Minutes of the meeting held on 08th May 2022

The meeting started at 1900hrs GMT with Vina, Beth, Samuel and Jubet in attendance

The meeting was basically to discuss the Development Team Project: Executive Summary in Unit 11.

Beth shared a Template for the Executive summary an gave a brief description of the design Template highlighting the requirements for the Exercise

Vina complained on inability to access the site allmytype.co.uk on a few occasions while trying to perform scans

Samuel pointed out that the Nikto scan done by Vina in the last exercise can suffice for the vulnerability identification of the site allmytype.co.uk. The scan featured open ports vulnerabilities, no HTTP X-XSS-Protection etc

Not all vulnerabilities will be included however, justification will be provided for that

The Vulnerabilities identified will be categorised. For example vulnerabilities like open ports already identified in the last Exercise will be treated as under Security Mis-configuration, improper error handling also under Security mis-configuration
